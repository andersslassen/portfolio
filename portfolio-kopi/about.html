About.

I am an innovative 20 year old independent multimedia designer working with everything
ranging from graphic design, photography, video editing, webdesign, print design and
communication. I have a strong work ethic and because of that I am always 100%
dedicated to the project I am working with. I strive to evolve on a personal level and attempt
to expand my skillset as every day passes. I love the diversity when working with design
projects in teams, just as much as I like the freedom when working on a project on my own.

Personal life.

Currently I am living in a city called Odense which is Denmarks third largest city. It is
located on a small island called Fyn, situated between Jylland and Sjælland. I recently
moved from Sjælland to Fyn because I wanted to study multimedia design, which is a
big passion of mine. When I am not busy studying, I am probably at work at IKEA, where
I work with customer service.

In my freetime I love to listen to hiphop which I have had a big interest in since I was
a little kid. Besides that I like playing or watching football in the television or even playing
some FIFA on my Playstation. At the weekends (if I'm not working or have to study the
next day) you can always find me at one of Odenses bars or nightclubs having a good
time with my friends.
